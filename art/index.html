ART

My paintings are acrylic on canvas. I am moved and motivated by surrealistic art - dreamlike imagery... real objects in an unrealistic setting. I enjoy painting in fine detail with small brushes rather than making wide and generous brush strokes.

COFFEE SPLASH, 2019

Coffee cups filled with coffee on a coffee bean splashing into a puddle of coffee. Inspired from Nespresso's PIXIE collection. Yes, I like coffee ;)

MUSICALITY, 2021

Why is it that many songs from the 70's, 80's and 90's are all so unique and leaves an imprint in our brain, while the music nowadays is staring to sound the same and short-lived and... "burns up" (as this painting tries to depict)? Billy Hume shares some interesting perspectives on this topic.

FLUIDITY, 2023

Fluid Art Workshop
"Acrylic Pouring (auch Acrylgiessen genannt) ist eine weitere sehr beliebte Fluid Art Technik, mit der faszinierende abstrakte Kunst erzeugt werden kann. Dabei wird Acrylfarbe mit speziellem Pouring Medium gemischt, um die richtige Konsistenz für Giessen und Manipulieren zu erhalten. Danach werden verschiedene Techniken verwendet, um das Bild zu gestalten (Schwerkraft, Fönen, Hitze)."
